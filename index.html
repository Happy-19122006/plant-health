Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about CropGuard AI

What crops and diseases does CropGuard AI support?

CropGuard AI supports 25+ crop types including rice, wheat, maize, tomato, potato, and more. We can detect 50+ diseases with high accuracy. Check our detailed Supported Crops & Diseases section for the complete list.

How accurate is the disease detection?

Our model achieves 99.2% accuracy on validation sets and 94.8% in real field conditions. We use 5-fold cross-validation and field testing with research partners like ICAR and IARI.

Is CropGuard AI free to use?

Yes, CropGuard AI is completely free for farmers and agricultural professionals. We believe in democratizing access to AI-powered crop disease detection.

How do I take good photos for disease detection?

Take 3 photos: 1) Close-up of affected area, 2) Full plant view, 3) Underside of leaves. Ensure good lighting, sharp focus, and simple background. Avoid blurry or dark photos.

What if the AI can't detect the disease?

If detection fails, try retaking photos with better lighting and focus. You can also provide feedback about the correct disease to help improve our model.

How long does analysis take?

Analysis typically takes 15-30 seconds. We show real-time progress updates and optimize for low bandwidth connections.

Does CropGuard AI work offline?

We're working on offline capabilities. Currently, you need internet connection, but we optimize for low bandwidth and slow connections.

What devices are supported?

CropGuard AI works on all devices - smartphones, tablets, and computers. It's optimized for mobile use in field conditions.

How is my data protected?

All images are encrypted with AES-256 encryption. Photos are deleted after 30 days unless you save them. We never share your data with third parties.

Can I delete my images immediately?

Yes, you can delete your images anytime by contacting us or using the delete option in your account settings.
